# First 60 Minutes Checklist

## Stabilize

- Stop signing transactions from suspected devices.
- Identify affected wallets, chains, and assets.
- Preserve transaction hashes and screenshots.
- Separate compromised wallets from unaffected wallets.

## Contain

- Move unaffected assets only from a clean device and verified wallet.
- Revoke risky approvals where safe and relevant.
- Pause automated operations that may keep using compromised credentials.
- Notify internal stakeholders through a secure channel.

## Preserve

- Record timestamps in UTC.
- Save explorer links.
- Keep phishing URLs, messages, and emails with sensitive data redacted.
- Document who took each action and when.

## Escalate

- Prepare an exchange or platform escalation package when funds touch a service provider.
- Consult legal counsel for high-value or regulated incidents.
- Avoid public claims that are not supported by evidence.
