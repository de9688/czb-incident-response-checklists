# Phishing Approval Response Checklist

## Confirm

- Identify the approved spender contract.
- Confirm token and network.
- Check whether assets are still at risk.
- Record the approval transaction hash.

## Respond

- Revoke or reduce approval where safe.
- Move unaffected assets from a clean environment.
- Record any follow-on transactions.
- Preserve phishing page evidence with sensitive data removed.

## Report

- Report phishing domains through appropriate abuse channels.
- Share indicators with internal teams.
- Publish public warnings only after verifying the domain, contract, or pattern.
