# Wallet Compromise Checklist

## Identify The Failure Mode

- Seed phrase or private key exposure
- Malicious approval
- Compromised browser extension
- Compromised device
- Social engineering or impersonation
- Exchange account compromise

## Data To Collect

- Wallet addresses
- Chain/network
- Transaction hashes
- Token contracts
- Approval records
- Suspected phishing domains or messages

## Defensive Notes

- Never share seed phrases or private keys for investigation.
- Do not reuse the compromised wallet for future signing.
- Treat unknown wallet software and browser extensions as high risk until verified.
