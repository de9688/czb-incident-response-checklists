# Incident Communications

## Internal Updates

- Use one secure channel for incident coordination.
- Record decisions and timestamps.
- Separate confirmed facts from working hypotheses.

## External Updates

- Avoid publishing wallet owner personal data.
- Avoid naming an attacker unless evidence is strong and legally reviewed.
- Provide user-protection guidance before broad speculation.

## Public Statement Structure

- What happened
- What is confirmed
- What users should do
- What remains under review
- Where updates will be posted
